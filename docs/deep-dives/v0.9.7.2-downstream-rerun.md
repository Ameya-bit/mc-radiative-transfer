## 1. What A3 does

The production library ships **5 independent per-seed I(μ; τ) curves** (`intensity_by_tau_seed`).
Because the anchor pipeline is pure interpolation + geometry — no Monte Carlo — pushing each
seed's curve through the *same* verified machinery that makes the pooled headline gives the
**seed-to-seed spread of the observable**, i.e. the error bar on ΔPF in the currency of the
paper's claim. That is the whole point of the 5-seed design: the number now comes with a σ.

`scripts/a3_seed_errors.py` reuses `j0740_anchor.sweep_anchor` and `j0030_anchor.sweep_anchor`
**unchanged** — a single seed's `intensity_by_tau_seed[:, si, :]` slice already has the
(n_tau, n_bins) shape they expect. The four downstream scripts were also re-run as-is off the
new pooled library to regenerate their figures and `.npz`.

## 2. The headline — ΔPF ± σ

**J0740 (non-eclipsing → the systematic lands in the pulsed fraction):**

| anchor | ΔPF(τ = 10) ± σ | prior (200k single realization) |
|---|---|---|
| **Riley 2021 (X-PSI ST-U)** | **+0.1437 ± 0.0032** | +0.164 |
| **Miller 2021 (Illinois–Maryland)** | **+0.2012 ± 0.0052** | +0.229 |

**J0030 (eclipsing → PF saturates, systematic hides in waveform shape):**

| anchor | peak shape-RMS ± σ (τ = 1) | ΔPF |
|---|---|---|
| Riley 2019 (X-PSI ST+PST) | 0.0619 ± 0.0010 | ≈ 0 (PF pinned at 1) |
| Miller 2019 (Illinois–Maryland) | 0.0605 ± 0.0016 | ≈ 0 (PF pinned at 1) |

The **+0.16 → +0.15 shift v0.9.7 predicted is confirmed**: the old +0.164 was a single 200k
realization sitting ~1σ high; the production number settles to **+0.144 ± 0.003**. Miller shifts
proportionally (+0.229 → +0.201), as expected from feeding off the same library rows. Note ΔPF(τ)
is a *plateau*, not a peak — it is marginally larger at τ = 30 (+0.147 Riley) than at τ = 10, but
the τ = 10 convention is retained as the headline (it sits on the saturated b(τ) plateau).

## 3. Sanity gates — all pass

1. **ΔPF(τ = 10) reproduces the v0.9.7 reference.** Riley +0.1437 ± 0.0032 vs the converged
   +0.1486 ± 0.0026 → **1.2σ, PASS**. The small downward nudge is consistent with the 8a/8b
   extraction change (v0.9.7.1 §3), not a convergence failure.
2. **b(τ) rises monotonically to a plateau** (τ = 10 ≡ τ = 30) — from v0.9.7.1 §3.
3. **τ = 10 I(μ) sits inside the [H(μ), Eddington] envelope** for 100% of μ > 0.1 bins
   (at μ = 0.5: MC 0.698, between H 0.692 and Eddington 0.700).

The supporting physics also re-ran clean off the new library and held every verdict:

- **`finite_cap.py`:** Riley ΔPF point +0.144 / finite-cap +0.140 (shift −0.004, refinement
